About Us:

Many years of experience allow us to implement many not only prestigious, but also technically advanced projects. We are a construction company specializing in the construction of shells of building structures mainly in Lower Silesia region and implementation of grain warehouses - silos throughout Poland. We started our activity in 2015 in Wroclaw.

Our mission

Our mission is to offer comprehensive construction services at the highest level. Our work is based on reliability, punctuality and a partnership approach to cooperation with clients and contractors. Each, even the smallest, project we implement is carried out in accordance with the principles of construction art. The services we provide are in accordance with the law and the contract concluded with the investor. The highest level of services is guaranteed by experienced staff with extensive construction and technical knowledge.
